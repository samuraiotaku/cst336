What is Unreal-Engine?


Unreal-Engine, like unity is a Game Engine used for developing video games and simulations.

It uses a pure C++ Engine that was design for high performance.

It is a complex tool designed to meet artistic visions, and flexible enough to be used by teams of all sizes.
